E-commerce

Electronic commerce, commonly known as E-commerce or eCommerce, is trading in products or services, electronic funds transfer, supply chain management, Internet marketing, online transaction processing, electronic data interchange (EDI), inventory managment systems, and automated data collection systems.

Modern electronic commerce typicall uses the World Wide Web for at least one paet of the transaction's life cycle, although it may also use other technologies such as email, mobile devices, social media, and telephones.
